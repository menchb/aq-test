What is Adversity Quotient (AQ)

"An adversity quotient is a person's ability to manage difficulties and transform obstacles into opportunities."

(Hurlock, 2000 as cited in Safi'i, Muttaqin, Sukino, Hamzah, Chotimah, Junaris and Rifa'i, 2021)

It is one of the four main intelligence types, the other three being Intelligence Quotient (IQ), Emotional Quotient (EQ), and Social Quotient (SQ).
