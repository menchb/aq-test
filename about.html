AQ Test

Raise self-awareness.

AQ Test is an open-source project hosted in GitHub, starting on July 27, 2022. It started as the creator's personal project, but contributions were offered, thus growing the project further. We took on the challenge to continually add value to AQ Test, all in the motivation of helping people build their resilience.

The Adversity Quotient test by Paul Stoltz, Ph.D. is displayed for people to take and find out their scores. Explanation is also given after the test to help them understood the core divisions of their adversity response, whether to learn how to improve or maintain them.

Further in the website's roadmap, we shall integrate credible blogposts for people to read regarding their adversity quotient.

We hear your silent battles.
